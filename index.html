The #1 Resource for Veterans Seeking Employment in the Civil Service

Thank you for your service. This website is here to help veterans like you find employment in local, state and federal government. Here you will find all the resources you need to figure out the ins and outs of the civil service job hunt. This includes detailed guidance on how to get the veteran's preference you earned, how to prepare your resume for the job you are looking for and how and where to apply.
